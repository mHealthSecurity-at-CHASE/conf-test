2025 International Conference on Intelligent Multimedia, Networking, and Security  

July 5-6th, 2025, Qingdao, China

 

   Registration

 

    HOW TO REGISTER


         Registration must be completed, and payment received by 30 May 2025 via the link below:

         Click “Registration” 

 

    REGISTRATION FEEs

         Full registration (Author): $700
         Full registration (non-Author) $400

 

         Full conference registration fees include access to conference sessions and exhibit hall, networking events, coffee breaks, lunches and electronic proceedings.

        

        Notes:

        Paper Uploads: To submit the final paper and all associated attachments, at least one of the authors must register in advance. 

        Authors who pay an author registration fee are allowed to upload up to 2 papers.

 

    METHOD OF PAYMENT

        Registration can only be confirmed once payment is received.
        Payment can be made by any of the following methods:
            * Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)


    CANCELLATION POLICY

        The cancellation will only  be made in exceptional circumstances with $80 service fee. 

        Failure to obtain the appropriate visa to travel does not constitute a refund. 

        The name on the registration can be changed without penalty.
