Overview
The International Conference on Intelligent Multimedia, Networking, and Security (IMNS 2025) invites researchers, academics, and industry professionals to submit original and high-quality papers addressing cutting-edge topics in the fields of multimedia systems, robust and secure networking architectures, and innovative security solutions for the AI age. This conference aims to foster interdisciplinary research and collaboration to explore the latest advancements and challenges in these rapidly evolving domains, especially in the era of AI.

Conference Topics
IMNS 2024 seeks papers that address theoretical, experimental, and applied aspects of intelligent multimedia, networking, and security. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  1. Intelligent Multimedia Systems

    • Multimedia content analysis and understanding

    • AI and deep learning techniques in multimedia processing

    • Multimedia information retrieval and search

    • Augmented and virtual reality applications

    • Image, audio, and video processing

    • Human-computer interaction in multimedia

    • Multimedia data compression and transmission

    • Intelligent multimedia in healthcare, education, and entertainment

  2. Next-Generation Networking Technologies

    • 5G/6G networks and beyond

    • Software-defined networking (SDN)

    • Internet of Things (IoT) and smart systems

    • Network performance optimization

    • Wireless and mobile networks

    • Network virtualization and cloud computing

    • Quality of service (QoS) and Quality of Experience (QoE)

    • Network traffic analysis and management

    • Edge and fog computing for intelligent systems

  3. Cybersecurity and Privacy

    • Network security and threat mitigation

    • Cryptography and secure communication

    • Blockchain applications in security

    • Privacy-preserving algorithms and protocols

    • AI and machine learning for cybersecurity

    • Intrusion detection and prevention systems

    • Security in IoT and cloud computing

    • Cyber risk management and resilience

    • Ethical issues in cybersecurity and data privacy

  4. Interdisciplinary Topics

    • Integration of AI with multimedia, networking, and security

    • Smart cities and intelligent systems

    • Autonomous systems and cybersecurity

    • Data-driven approaches for multimedia, networking, and security

    • Sustainable and energy-efficient networking

    • Real-time systems and edge intelligence

    • Case studies and real-world applications of intelligent multimedia, networking, and security

 

Submission Guidelines

  • Paper Submission System: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IMNS2025

  • Paper Length: Papers should be no longer than eight (8) pages in length, including references. Each extra page will be charged at $100.

  • Format: All submissions must be formatted according to the IEEE conference template.

  • Submission Platform: Papers should be submitted via the conference’s online submission system (link to be provided).

  • Review Process: All submitted papers will undergo a peer review process.

  • Accepted Papers: Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings in the IEEE Xplore database and may be selected for inclusion in special issues of high-impact journals.
