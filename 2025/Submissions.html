2025 International Conference on Intelligent Multimedia, Networking, and Security  

July 5-6th, 2025, Qingdao, China

 

Submission Guidelines

  • Paper Submission System: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IMNS2025

  • Paper Length: Papers should be no longer than eight (8) pages in length, including references. Each extra page will be charged at $100.

  • Format: All submissions must be formatted according to the IEEE conference template.

  • Submission Platform: Papers should be submitted via the conference’s online submission system (link to be provided).

  • Review Process: All submitted papers will undergo a peer review process.

  • Accepted Papers: Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings in the IEEE Xplore database and may be selected for inclusion in special issues of high-impact journals.
