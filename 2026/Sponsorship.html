IEEE IMNS 2026 Sponsorship

Bronze Level (USD)

Amount: ≥ $2,500

Benefits

  • Your company may set up a table (up to 2' × 4') for demonstration at the conference site.

Silver Level (USD)

Amount: ≥ $3,500

Benefits

  • All Bronze Level benefits
  • Company logo displayed on the event website
  • Company logo included in IMNS Conference Proceedings and final printed advance program
  • Company name included in all web and email announcements
  • Company name listed in acknowledgments during opening remarks
  • Company publications (maximum of 2 pages) inserted into conference bags
  • One complimentary registration

Gold Level (USD)

Amount: ≥ $5,000

Benefits

  • All Silver Level benefits
  • Space for distributing company recruitment advertisements
  • One additional complimentary registration (two total)

Platinum Level (USD)

Amount: ≥ $7,500

Benefits

  • All Gold Level benefits
  • Company logo included on all event printed materials

Diamond Level (USD)

Amount: ≥ $10,000

Benefits

  • All Platinum Level benefits
  • Opportunity to deliver a 10-minute presentation during reception or lunch
  • One additional complimentary registration (three total)


Sponsorship Information

IEEE IMNS 2026 accepts the above levels of sponsorships with corresponding benefits. Interested organizations should contact the conference finance chair. Additional benefits beyond those listed may be discussed with the finance chair and executive chair.

A sponsorship between a company or organization and IEEE IMNS 2026 does not imply endorsement of each other’s agendas, programs, or products.

Agreement and payment deadline: May 31, 2026
