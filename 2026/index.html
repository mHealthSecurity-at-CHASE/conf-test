International Conference on Intelligent Multimedia, Networking, and Security

The International Conference on Intelligent Multimedia, Networking, and Security is a global forum that brings together researchers, academics, and industry experts to exchange knowledge and advancements in multimedia technologies, communication networks, and cybersecurity. This conference highlights cutting-edge research and innovative solutions addressing the challenges in intelligent multimedia processing, secure networking protocols, and data protection techniques. The event fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, offering keynote speeches, technical sessions, workshops, and panel discussions to promote the latest trends and practical implementations in the field.
